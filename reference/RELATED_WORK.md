# Related Work And Research Sources

File nay dung de gom paper, repo, dataset va tai lieu lien quan cho huong nghien cuu moi.

## Core Papers

- Wu et al. (2025), `(Perhaps) Beyond Human Translation: Harnessing Multi-Agent Collaboration for Translating Ultra-Long Literary Texts`, TACL 2025.
  - Local PDF: `papers/2025.tacl-1.42.pdf`
  - ACL Anthology: https://aclanthology.org/2025.tacl-1.42/
  - Vai tro: related work chinh cho multi-agent literary translation, MHP/BLP/GEMBA-DA/MATTR/MTLD, va bai hoc guideline dai co the gay nhieu.
- Wang et al. (2024), `DelTA: An Online Document-Level Translation Agent Based on Multi-Level Memory`.
  - Vai tro: related work cho document-level translation agent va multi-level memory.
- Lewis et al. (2020), `Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP`.
  - Vai tro: nen tang RAG/retrieval.
- Liu et al. (2024), `Lost in the Middle: How Language Models Use Long Contexts`.
  - Vai tro: ly do khong dua toan bo context dai vao prompt.
